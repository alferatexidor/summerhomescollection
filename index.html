Tips from
the owners

France — Food
Drive to Brantôme on a Tuesday

The Tuesday market is quieter than Périgueux and the charcuterie stall near the abbey has the best duck rillettes in the region. Arrive by 9am.

→
France — Activities
The Lascaux IV cave complex

The replica caves are genuinely extraordinary — not a consolation prize. Book morning slots on weekdays to avoid coach parties. Children are often more moved than adults.

→
Thailand — Beach
Silver Beach before 8am

Silver Beach is only accessible by foot or longtail boat. Before 8am it's completely empty. The water is clear and shallow — perfect for children and nervous swimmers.

→
Thailand — Food
Lamai night market, not Chaweng

Lamai's night market is smaller, less touristy and the food is better. Try the pad see ew stall run by a mother and daughter — third from the left at the entrance.

→
France — Day Trip
The medieval village of Domme

Perched above the Dordogne river, Domme has one of the finest viewpoints in Southwest France. Go late afternoon when the light falls across the valley.

→
Thailand — Culture
Wat Plai Laem at sunrise

The temple complex on the north shore is best visited at first light, before the tour boats arrive. Bring an offering and take your shoes off — even on the outside steps.
